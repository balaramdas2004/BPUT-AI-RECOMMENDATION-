# BPUT CareerAI - Sustainability & Business Model Plan

## Executive Summary

BPUT CareerAI is designed as a financially sustainable platform that bridges the gap between students, educational institutions, and employers through AI-powered career guidance and placement facilitation. This document outlines our comprehensive revenue strategy, operational model, and long-term sustainability plan.

---

## 1. Revenue Model Overview

### Multi-Tier Subscription Strategy

Our platform operates on a **freemium subscription model** with three distinct customer segments:

1. **Students** - Free & Premium tiers
2. **Employers** - Free & Premium tiers  
3. **Institutions** - Enterprise licensing

## 2. Detailed Pricing Strategy

### 2.1 Student Plans

#### Free Tier (Target: 10,000+ users)
- **Price:** ₹0
- **Features:**
  - Basic profile creation
  - 10 job applications/month
  - 20 AI assistant queries/month
  - Access to job board
  - Community features
  - Career readiness score (monthly)

**Conversion Strategy:** 5% conversion to Premium

#### Premium Student (Target: 500 users in Year 1)
- **Monthly:** ₹199/month
- **Annual:** ₹1,999/year (17% savings)
- **Features:**
  - All Free features
  - Unlimited job applications
  - Unlimited AI career assistant
  - Priority in employer search (3x visibility)
  - Resume ATS optimizer
  - 5 mock interviews/month with AI feedback
  - Skill verification certificates
  - Weekly career readiness updates
  - LinkedIn profile optimization
  - Early access to new jobs (24h priority)

**Revenue Impact Year 1:** 500 users × ₹1,999 = ₹9,99,500

## 7. Risk Mitigation

### 7.1 Financial Risks

**Risk:** Low conversion rates from free to premium
**Mitigation:**
- A/B testing on pricing
- Limited-time offers
- Enhanced free tier restrictions
- Social proof and testimonials

**Risk:** High customer acquisition costs
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on organic growth through institutions
- Referral program incentives
- Content marketing
- Strategic partnerships

### 7.2 Technical Risks

**Risk:** AI API cost escalation
**Mitigation:**
- Usage caps on free tier
- Bulk pricing negotiations with Lovable AI
- Caching strategies
- Fallback to rule-based systems

**Risk:** Platform scalability issues
**Mitigation:**
- Cloud-native architecture (Lovable Cloud)
- Regular performance audits
- Load testing
- Auto-scaling infrastructure

### 7.3 Market Risks

**Risk:** Competitor entry
**Mitigation:**
- Strong institutional partnerships
- Continuous innovation
- Data moat (placement insights)
- Network effects

**Risk:** Economic downturn affecting hiring
**Mitigation:**
- Diversified revenue streams
- Freemium buffer
- Institutional contracts (sticky)
- Skill development focus (recession-proof)
